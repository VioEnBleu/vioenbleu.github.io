Allie AI Assistant

Your intelligent companion for everyday tasks

Allie is an advanced AI assistant designed to help users with various tasks including information retrieval, codding, and simple accademic knowledge.

Key features Tech Stack

Key Features

Natural Conversations

Engage in human-like dialogues with context awareness and emotional intelligence for more meaningful interactions.

Deeper explainations

Ask for details, ideas and solutions, ways to improves or just projets to do. She is always creative.

Learning System

Adapts to your preferences and behavior with a simple prompt, providing increasingly personalized assistance.

Well Know Model

Allie is using the Llama 8b, a world class LLM produced by Meta, direct rival of GPT 3.5 Turbo.

Privacy Focused

Local processing and no server to stock your data. As simple as that. All in your SSD.

(Soon) Visual Ecosystem

[In dev state] A good looking avatar in 3d and a customizable voice for more personalized experiences.

Development Highlights

Architecture

Modular design with separate components for speech recognition, natural language understanding, dialogue management, and response generation. Microservices architecture allows for scalability.

Performance

Optimized response times under 500ms for most queries. Local processing option reduces latency and improves privacy for sensitive requests.

Offline

No need to be in radius of any internet, Allie is on my computer all day long. Don't worry, she can watch youtube sometimes.
